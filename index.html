Getting Started

Planting Use seeds on empty dirt tiles to grow crops Harvesting Collect crops when fully grown Selling Sell crops for Sheckles, the in-game currency Upgrading Use Sheckles to buy better seeds, gear, and expand your farm

Weather and Mutations

Sunny Normal growth Rain Crops grow 50% faster and may develop the Wet mutation (double value) Thunderstorm Can trigger the Shocked mutation (50x value)

Crop Mutations

Normal Standard crops Big Larger, more valuable crops Wet Grows in rain, doubles value Golden 1% chance, 20x value Shocked From thunderstorms, 50x value Rainbow 0.1% chance, 50x value

Gear and Tools

Watering Can Speeds up growth Sprinklers Increase growth and mutation odds Trowel Move plants Shovel Remove plants Lightning Rod Increases chance for Shocked mutation

Pets

Provide farming boosts Examples include Dog (+15% harvest) Bee (+25% reproduction) Fox (+30% selling price)

Strategies

Use mutations to boost profits Play during storms for higher mutation chances Use gear effectively Team up with friends for cash bonuses.
